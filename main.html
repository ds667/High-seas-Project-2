What is a Laptop?

Laptops are portable computers designed for personal, business, and creative use. They combine the functionality of a desktop in a compact, easy-to-carry design.

Types of Laptops

Gaming Laptops

High-performance laptops with powerful GPUs, CPUs, and high-refresh-rate displays.

Ultrabooks

Lightweight and slim laptops with long battery life, ideal for portability.

2-in-1 Laptops

Laptops that double as tablets, featuring touchscreens and flexible hinges.

Business Laptops

Designed for professionals, offering durability, security, and productivity features.

Popular Laptop Companies

  • Apple: Known for premium MacBooks with macOS.
  • Dell: Offers reliable and durable laptops like the XPS and Inspiron series.
  • HP: Features versatile laptops for home, business, and gaming needs.
  • Lenovo: Popular for ThinkPad and IdeaPad series catering to all users.

Laptop Company Comparisons

Best Laptops

Least Expensive Laptops

Acer: Offers budget-friendly laptops with decent performance for everyday tasks.

Most Expensive Laptops

Apple: The MacBook Pro (customized versions) can cost upwards of $6,000 due to high-end configurations.

Highest Net Value

Apple: With a net worth exceeding $2.8 trillion, Apple leads the industry in innovation and premium products.
